A huge, pixel art MMO.

Jump in, loot up, log out



Soulbound is a free-to-play pixel-art action-MMO that blends bullet-hell combat, roguelike loot-runs and a 100% player-driven economy. Instant-load in any modern browser – no clients, no patches.


Forge theory-crafted builds, own land, run your blacksmith shop and climb seasonal ladders – all at your pace.


Build your perfect
play-style. Tank, healer, DPS or hybrid

Create and personalize your character with endless options



Mix more than 100 swappable abilities, multiple weapon archetypes and deep skill trees to craft truly unique builds. Instantly respec to fit the fight, then flex hard-earned pixel cosmetics – never pay-to-win.


Start your guild today.

Team up with player owned land.



Build your own guild base, craft new weapons, gear and potions and share access to your friends or guild mates.


Bullet-hell roguelite combat

Fast paced dungeon combat



Instanced runs scale for solo or up-to-three-player co-op. Storm-spawned mobs, procedurally-escalating boss phases and full bag-loss on death keep every run fresh – while permanent meta-progression lets you grow stronger between attempts.
